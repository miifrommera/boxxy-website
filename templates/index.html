Getting Started Development

Native Nightlies (Requires GTK 4.22+)

curl -sSf https://raw.githubusercontent.com/boxxy-dev/boxxy/main/scripts/install.sh | sh

Flatpak Stable (Recommended)

curl -O https://boxxy-dev.github.io/boxxy-flatpak-remote/boxxy.gpg && \
  flatpak remote-add --user --if-not-exists --gpg-import=boxxy.gpg \
  boxxy https://boxxy-dev.github.io/boxxy-flatpak-remote/repo && \
  flatpak install --user boxxy dev.boxxy.BoxxyTerminal

Not just an app on the terminal. It is the terminal.

Most AI coding tools are trapped in your editor or run as isolated CLI commands. Boxxy is a terminal emulator that natively understands your environment. It watches your workflow, catches your errors, and can autonomously fix broken dependencies or manage system configurations using the tools you already have installed.

Just press Control + / and you're ready to go!

High Performance

A fast, fully-featured Linux terminal built for efficiency. It's a great daily driver, even before you turn the AI on.

Agentic Core

Powered by BoxxyClaw, your terminal understands your intent, remembers preferences, and assists your workflow.

Total Privacy

Connect to local LLMs like Ollama to keep your code, data, and terminal history entirely on your machine.
